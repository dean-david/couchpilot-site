Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 3, 2026

CouchPilot ("the app") is a TV and movie tracking app with optional social features.

i.What we collect

Without an account: nothing. Your watch history lives only on your device. Show and movie metadata is fetched from TMDB; those requests include your IP address, as any web request does.

With an account (optional):

  • Your email address (for sign-in) — or, with Sign in with Apple, the private relay address Apple gives us
  • Your chosen username, display name, and optional bio
  • Your watch history, ratings, and reactions (synced so you can restore them and share activity with people who follow you)
  • Comments you post and likes you give

ii.What we don't do

  • No ads, no ad tracking, no analytics SDKs, no selling data
  • We never post anywhere on your behalf
  • Episode comments are visible only to users who have also marked that episode watched

iii.Where data lives

Account data is stored with Supabase (hosted PostgreSQL). Show and movie metadata comes from TMDB; where-to-watch data comes from JustWatch via TMDB.

iv.Deleting your data

Settings → Account → Delete account permanently removes your profile, watch history, comments, likes, follows, and login — immediately and in-app, no email required. Your local on-device library is yours and remains.

v.Export

Settings → Your data lets you export everything as JSON or CSV at any time.
