i.Common questions

How do I import my history from TV Time?

Settings → Import → pick your TV Time export file. CouchPilot reads both the official GDPR data export (the zip TV Time emails you) and JSON/CSV files from browser-extension exporters. Shows, episodes, movies, rewatches, and your original watch dates all come across. Importing the same file twice is safe — nothing gets double-counted.

Why is an episode's discussion locked?

Every episode has a Screening Room that only opens after you've marked that episode watched. That's the whole idea: you can't be spoiled by a thread you can't see. Check in to the episode and the room unlocks.

Do I need an account?

No. Tracking works entirely on your device with no sign-in. An account (email or Sign in with Apple) is only needed for the social layer — syncing, following friends, and joining Screening Rooms.

How do I export my data?

Settings → Your data → Export. Everything you've tracked leaves as JSON or CSV, any time, no account required.

How do I delete my account?

Settings → Account → Delete account. It takes effect immediately, in the app — your profile, watch history, comments, likes, and follows are removed from our servers and your Sign in with Apple token is revoked. Your local on-device library stays on your phone.

How do I report a comment or block someone?

Tap the ⋯ menu on any comment to report it or block its author. Reports are reviewed within 24 hours, and anything reported by three people is hidden automatically pending review. Your blocked list lives in Settings.
